Privacy Policy for XRechnung to PDF Viewer
Last Updated: March 27, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how XRechnung to PDF Viewer ("the Application") handles information when you use the software.

1. No Data Collection
The Application is designed as a standalone, offline utility.

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

We do not track your usage, location, or device information.

There are no analytics, cookies, or third-party tracking tools embedded in the software.

2. Processing of Invoice Data
The Application processes electronic invoices (XML files in XRechnung, ZUGFeRD, or Factur-X formats).

Local Processing: All data parsing and PDF generation occur entirely on your local device.

No Cloud Uploads: Your financial data is never uploaded to a server, cloud service, or any third party.

Temporary Memory: Data is held in your computer's volatile memory (RAM) only while the Application is open and is cleared upon closing.

3. Internet Connectivity
The Application does not require an internet connection to function. It does not communicate with any external APIs or databases.

4. Third-Party Services
Since the Application is distributed through the Microsoft Store, Microsoft may collect basic telemetry related to downloads and installations as governed by the Microsoft Privacy Statement. We do not have access to any personally identifiable information through these reports.

5. Contact Information
If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, you can contact the developer directly through the support channels provided in the Microsoft Store listing.
